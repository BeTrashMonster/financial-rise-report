## Launch Strategy

### Launch Approach

**Type:** Soft Launch â†’ Gradual Rollout â†’ Full Public Launch

**Timeline:**
- **Week 1:** UAT participants only (5-10 consultants)
- **Week 2-3:** Referrals from UAT participants (20-30 consultants)
- **Week 4:** Limited public launch (application-based)
- **Month 2:** Full public launch

### Target Audience

**Primary:** Fractional CFOs & Financial Consultants
- 2+ years experience
- Work with small-medium businesses (10-100 employees)
- Currently doing financial assessments manually
- Tech-savvy, early adopters

**Secondary:** Accountants, Bookkeepers, Financial Advisors

### Value Proposition

**One-Line Pitch:**
"Financial RISE helps consultants assess client financial readiness in 30 minutes and generate personalized, DISC-adapted action plans instantly."

**Key Benefits:**
1. **Save Time:** 30-45 min assessment vs. 3-4 hours manual
2. **DISC Integration:** Automatically adapt reports to client communication style
3. **Professional Reports:** Dual PDF reports (consultant + client versions)
4. **Proven Framework:** 5-phase financial readiness methodology
5. **Actionable Insights:** Specific, prioritized recommendations

### Success Metrics

**Month 1 Targets:**
- 50 registered consultants
- 100 assessments created
- 80 assessments completed
- 10 testimonials collected

**Month 3 Targets:**
- 200 registered consultants
- 500 assessments created
- 400 assessments completed
