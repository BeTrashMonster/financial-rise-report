## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- TLS 1.2+ encryption for data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest
- Secure authentication (JWT with bcrypt password hashing)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Automated vulnerability scanning

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- Limited employee access on need-to-know basis
- Regular security training
- Incident response procedures
- Third-party security certifications

**Infrastructure Security:**
- AWS cloud infrastructure with security certifications
- Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
- Regular backups with encryption
- Intrusion detection and prevention

Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations:

**Active Accounts:**
- Account data: Duration of account + 90 days after deletion request
- Assessment data: Duration of account + 7 years (for compliance)
- Usage logs: 90 days

**Inactive Accounts:**
- Accounts inactive for 24+ months may be deleted after notice
- Assessment data retained for 7 years (compliance requirement)

**Legal Holds:**
- Data subject to legal hold retained until hold is lifted
- Compliance-required data retained per applicable law

**Deletion Requests:**
- Account data deleted within 30 days of request
- Backups containing deleted data purged within 90 days
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance
