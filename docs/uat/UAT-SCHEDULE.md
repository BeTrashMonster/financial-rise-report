## Contingency Plans

### If Pilot Drops Out
- **Trigger:** Pilot becomes unavailable or stops participating
- **Response:**
  - Reach out to understand issue
  - If unrecoverable, continue with remaining pilots
  - If drops below 6 pilots, recruit backup from waitlist

### If Critical Bug Blocks Testing
- **Trigger:** P0 bug prevents pilots from completing assessments
- **Response:**
  - All-hands dev team triage (within 1 hour)
  - Pause UAT if needed
  - Fix and deploy ASAP
  - Communicate transparently with pilots
  - Extend UAT timeline if necessary

### If Low Survey Response Rate
- **Trigger:** <70% survey completion after 48 hours
- **Response:**
  - Send reminder email
  - Personal outreach via Slack DM
  - Extend survey deadline by 24 hours
  - Increase incentive (if needed)

### If Poor Interview Attendance
- **Trigger:** <60% interview scheduling
- **Response:**
  - Personal outreach to non-responders
  - Offer alternative times/formats
  - Make interviews optional, increase survey depth
  - Offer async video responses as alternative
