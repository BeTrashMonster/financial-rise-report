## Template 3: Exit Interview Script

**Purpose:** Conduct structured 30-minute interviews with pilot participants.

**Format:** Video call (Zoom/Google Meet), recorded with permission.

---

### Exit Interview Script

```
# Financial RISE Pilot - Exit Interview Script
**Participant Name:** _______________
**Date:** _______________
**Duration:** appropriate time
**Interviewer:** _______________

---

## Pre-Interview Setup (5 minutes before call)

- [ ] Review participant's survey responses
- [ ] Review participant's usage data (# assessments, completion rate)
- [ ] Review any bug reports or support tickets
- [ ] Prepare specific follow-up questions based on their feedback
- [ ] Test recording software
- [ ] Have note-taking template ready

---

## Introduction (2 minutes)

**Script:**
"Hi [Name], thanks so much for joining this exit interview and for participating in our pilot program. This call should take about appropriate time. I'd like to record this conversation so I can focus on our discussion rather than taking notesâ€”is that okay with you?"

[Wait for permission]

"Great, thank you. I've reviewed your survey responses, and I have a few follow-up questions to dive deeper into your experience. There are no right or wrong answersâ€”I'm just here to learn from your honest feedback."

---

## Section 1: Overall Impression (5 minutes)

1. **"Let's start big picture: What was your overall impression of Financial RISE?"**

   [Listen, probe for specific examples]

2. **"What did you like most about the product?"**

   [Note specific features they mention]

3. **"What frustrated you most or what would you change first if you could?"**

   [Probe: Why was that frustrating? How often did it happen?]

4. **"On your survey, you rated us [X/10] for overall satisfaction. Can you tell me more about that rating?"**

   [If low: "What would it take to get to a 9 or 10?"]
   [If high: "What specifically earned that high rating?"]

---

## Section 2: Feature-Specific Deep Dive (appropriate time)

### Questionnaire (3 minutes)

5. **"Let's talk about the questionnaire your clients completed. You rated questionnaire clarity [X/10]. Walk me through your client's experienceâ€”what worked well and what didn't?"**

   [Probe: Were any questions confusing? Too long? Missing?]

6. **"Did you notice the before/after confidence rating questions at the start and end? Were those useful?"**

7. **"Was the questionnaire length appropriate? About how long did it take your clients?"**

   [Compare to their expectation]

### DISC Profiling (3 minutes)

8. **"The consultant report included communication strategies based on your client's personality profile (DISC). Did you find those strategies helpful?"**

   [Probe: Did you actually use them? Were they accurate?]

9. **"The DISC profiling is intentionally hidden from clientsâ€”they don't see their 'D', 'I', 'S', 'C' scores. Do you think that was the right approach?"**

   [Alternative: Should clients see their DISC profile?]

### Phase Results (3 minutes)

10. **"The results page showed your client's primary financial phase (Stabilize, Organize, Build, Grow, or Systemic). Did that assessment align with your own understanding of where your client is at?"**

    [Probe: Were you surprised? Did it miss anything?]

11. **"Did the phase roadmap visual (showing all 5 phases) help you explain the path forward to your client?"**

### Reports (4 minutes)

12. **"Let's talk about the PDF reports. You rated report quality [X/10]. Would you share the client report with your client as-is, or would you want to edit it first?"**

    [If edit: "What specifically would you change?"]

13. **"Did the consultant report give you insights you didn't already know about your client?"**

14. **"How did your client react when you shared the report with them?"**

    [Probe: Did it increase engagement? Did they book a follow-up?]

15. **"Compared to your previous assessment deliverable (spreadsheet, email summary, etc.), how did Financial RISE reports compare in terms of professionalism?"**

---

## Section 3: Missing Features & Pain Points (5 minutes)

16. **"What features are you missing that would make Financial RISE more valuable to you?"**

    [Probe top 3 if they mention multiple]

17. **"If we added [FEATURE THEY MENTIONED], would that make Financial RISE a 'must-have' tool for you?"**

18. **"You mentioned [PAIN POINT FROM SURVEY]. Can you tell me more about that?"**

19. **"Is there anything that would prevent you from using Financial RISE even if we fixed everything you mentioned?"**

    [Dealbreaker check]

---

## Section 4: Pricing & Business Model (3 minutes)

20. **"On your survey, you indicated you'd be willing to pay [$X-$Y] per period. Can you walk me through that pricing decisionâ€”what were you comparing to?"**

21. **"At what price point would Financial RISE be a 'no-brainer' purchase for youâ€”where you'd subscribe immediately without hesitation?"**

22. **"Would you prefer a subscription subscription (unlimited assessments) or pay-per-assessment model?"**

    [Probe: How many assessments do you do per period?]

---

## Section 5: Testimonial & Advocacy (2 minutes)

23. **"Would you recommend Financial RISE to a colleague? Why or why not?"**

24. **[If positive feedback] "Would you be comfortable with us using a quote from you in our marketing materials? For example, something like '[QUOTE FROM EARLIER IN CALL]'?"**

    [Get explicit permission and exact quote]

---

## Wrap-Up (3 minutes)

25. **"Final question: Will you continue using Financial RISE after the pilot ends?"**

    [If yes: Great! We'll send details about paid plans]
    [If no: I understand. Is there anything we could do to change your mind?]

26. **"Is there anything else you'd like to share that we haven't covered?"**

27. **"Thank you so much for your time and incredibly valuable feedback. We're going to review all the pilot data this week and make decisions about our roadmap. I'll send you a summary of what we learned and how we're incorporating your feedback. And please feel free to reach out anytime if you think of anything else!"**
```
